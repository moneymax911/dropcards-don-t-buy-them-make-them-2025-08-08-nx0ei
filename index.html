About Dropcards

Founded in 2020, Dropcards emerged from a simple observation: small businesses needed an affordable, professional way to create marketing materials. Our founder, having spent years in the printing industry, saw how traditional printing services were often too expensive and inflexible for small business needs. This insight led to the development of our innovative DIY platform.

Today, our mission remains clear: to empower businesses of all sizes with the tools they need to create professional marketing materials without breaking the bank. We believe in transparency, creativity, and putting our customers first. Our values of innovation, accessibility, and excellence drive everything we do, from our user-friendly platform to our responsive customer support.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to create dropcards?

Our basic platform is free to use. You only pay for printing costs, which are typically 60-80% less than traditional printing services. Premium features and bulk printing discounts are available with our paid plans.

Do I need design experience to use Dropcards?

Not at all! Our platform includes easy-to-use templates and design tools that make it simple for anyone to create professional-looking marketing materials, regardless of their design experience.

What file formats can I use for my designs?

We support all major image formats including JPG, PNG, and PDF. You can also directly import designs from popular design tools or use our built-in design elements.

How long does it take to create and receive dropcards?

Design time varies by project, but most users complete their designs in under 30 minutes. Printing and delivery typically take 3-5 business days, with express options available.

Can I modify my designs after creating them?

Yes! All designs are saved to your account and can be modified at any time. Make updates, try different variations, or create new versions whenever you need.
